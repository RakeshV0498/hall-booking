

Render URL: http://hall-booking-89ie.onrender.com/

Endpoints: 

/rooms/all (GET) - Show all created rooms
/rooms/add (POST) - Add a new room
/booking/create-new (POST) - Book a room
/bookings/all (GET) - List all rooms with booked data
/bookings/customers-details (GET) - List all customers with booked data
/bookings/booking-details (GET) - List how many times a customer has booked the room with detailed information
